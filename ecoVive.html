EcoVive Collection

Project Overview

I designed high-fidelity wireframes for a sustainable fashion e-commerce website using Figma. The focus was on creating an intuitive, visually appealing interface that highlights eco-friendly products while providing a smooth and engaging shopping experience. I applied user-centered design principles, clear navigation flows, and interactive elements to ensure usability and showcase a modern, sustainable brand aesthetic.
