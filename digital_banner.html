Travelly Digital Banner

Project Overview

This project transforms a traditional business card into an interactive experience using Augmented Reality (AR). My AR Name Card — “The Muffin Man” combines graphic design with immersive technology to create something that is both personal and engaging. When viewed through a mobile device or webcam, the printed name card reveals a 3D muffin model that appears above the logo. This simple yet charming effect adds a layer of surprise and creativity to a typical name card, symbolizing warmth, craftsmanship, and the joy of making something with care. The project demonstrates how even small design elements can become interactive storytelling tools through AR.

Design Concept & Branding

The design concept is inspired by my fictional bakery brand, “The Muffin Man.” The idea was to design a name card that captures the brand’s friendly and comforting personality. The layout uses soft pastel tones and rounded typography to reflect sweetness and approachability — just like freshly baked goods. The logo and illustrations were created to feel lighthearted and inviting, allowing the overall design to communicate creativity and warmth. The muffin represents not just a baked treat, but also the brand’s identity — something handmade, thoughtful, and made with heart.

AR Implementation

Using Unity and Vuforia, I turned the static business card into an interactive experience. The printed name card serves as the image target, and upon scanning, a 3D muffin model appears above it. The muffin has a subtle rotation animation and lighting effects to enhance realism. This AR element not only adds depth to the design but also brings the brand’s personality to life in an unexpected, engaging way.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator: Designed the logo, icons, and name card layout.
  • Unity & Vuforia: Created and implemented the AR image tracking system.

Learning Outcome

Through this project, I learned how design and technology can work together to tell a story. It taught me the importance of consistency in branding, user experience, and optimization for AR performance. More importantly, it showed how creative technology can make even something as simple as a name card feel memorable and alive.
