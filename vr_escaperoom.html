The Last Memory

Project Overview

The Last Memory is an immersive virtual reality escape room experience that challenges players to solve puzzles and uncover mysteries in a surreal, memory-driven world. The game is set in a mysterious, dreamlike environment where players must navigate through various memory fragments, piecing together clues to unlock the story and escape before time runs out. Designed for both solo players and teams, the game combines atmospheric storytelling with interactive puzzles, offering a one-of-a-kind VR experience.

Technologies Used

  • Unity: Probuilder Tools
  • Unity Scripts: C#

Features

  • Puzzle Linking: Each room includes one main puzzle that unlocks the next space, guiding players through the story in a clear, emotional sequence.
  • Storytelling Through Objects: The narrative is discovered through meaningful items—old photos, books, clocks, letters—rather than cutscenes. Players piece together the great-grandfather’s identity by exploring the environment, making the story feel personal and interactive.
  • Natural VR-Based Puzzle Interaction: Puzzles rely on intuitive hand-tracked actions like turning books, opening cupboards, and pointing at clues. These realistic interactions enhance immersion and make solving puzzles feel physical rather than abstract.
  • Gloomy & Atmospheric VR Experience: Offers a calm, slow-paced environment with minimal UI, soft ambient sounds, and a mysterious visual tone. This meditative style sets it apart from fast-paced VR games and appeals to players who enjoy story-driven or relaxed puzzle experiences.
