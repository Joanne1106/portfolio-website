Volunteer Experience

Service Learning Student Volunteer

In September and October 2025, I volunteered to help seniors learn how to use TikTok and YouTube. During this experience, I guided them through creating accounts, navigating the platforms, and exploring content safely. It was incredibly rewarding to see their confidence grow as they learned new digital skills, and I really enjoyed the conversations and interactions that made the sessions both fun and meaningful. This experience also helped me develop patience, communication skills, and the ability to explain technology in simple, easy-to-understand ways.
