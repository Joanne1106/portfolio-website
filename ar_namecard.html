AR Name Card - The Muffin Man

Project Overview

This project transforms a traditional business card into an interactive AR experience for the fictional bakery brand, “The Muffin Man.” When scanned with a mobile device or webcam, the printed card reveals a 3D muffin model above the logo, adding a playful, memorable touch that reflects the brand’s friendly and comforting personality. Soft pastel tones, rounded typography, and lighthearted illustrations reinforce the design’s warmth and approachability.

Technologies Used

  • Canva
  • Unity & Vuforia
